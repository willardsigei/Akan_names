Akan Naming

Akan names come from a traditional naming system for
people from countries such as Togo, Ivory Coast and Ghana.
A childâ€™s name is determined by factors surrounding his or her birth.
For example, the day of their birth or the manner in which they were
born may determine which name they get irrespective of their gender.
These Akan names signify the day one is born. According to Akan tradition,
each child is born with a name associated to their soul. The day the child
is born is therefore determined by the soul.
